## What I Build

**AI Voice Receptionists** - Custom voice agents that answer every call on the first ring, handle conversations naturally, and take action (book appointments, send emails, update your CRM) without human intervention.

**Workflow Automation** - End-to-end n8n systems that connect your voice agent to calendars, CRMs, email, SMS, and any tool your business runs on. One call triggers an entire automated pipeline.

**Outbound AI Callers** - Voice agents that call your prospects, deliver your pitch, send demo links, and book discovery calls. When prospects call back, the system recognizes them and picks up where it left off.
